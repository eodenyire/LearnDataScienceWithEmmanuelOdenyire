# Discussion 01: Why Data Science?

## Prompt
Data science has been called "the sexiest job of the 21st century" (Harvard Business Review, 2012) and it continues to transform industries from healthcare to agriculture to entertainment.

**Respond to all three parts:**

### Part A: Your Personal Motivation (150+ words)
Why did you choose to learn data science? What specific problem or opportunity inspired you? How do you see data science fitting into your career or life goals?

### Part B: African Data Science (100+ words)
How do you think data science can be used to address challenges specific to Africa or your country? Identify **one concrete problem** where data science could make a significant difference. What data would be needed? What would success look like?

### Part C: Reply to a Classmate
Find a classmate's response where their problem area differs from yours. Write a 75-word reply that:
- Acknowledges one thing you found compelling about their answer
- Asks one genuine question to deepen the discussion

---

## Reflection Questions (Optional Self-Study)
1. What industries in your country are data-rich but underserved by data science?
2. What ethical considerations exist when applying data science in developing countries?
3. What is one dataset from your country that you wish existed?
