# Discussion 02: EDA Insights

## Prompt
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is the foundation of every data science project. It's where intuition, domain knowledge, and statistical thinking come together.

### Part A: Share Your Most Interesting EDA Finding
From the Netflix dataset analysis (or any dataset you've worked with):
- Describe **one surprising insight** you found during EDA
- Include a visualization (screenshot or description)
- Explain why this finding matters

### Part B: The "Aha Moment"
Describe a moment during EDA where you discovered something unexpected that changed your modeling approach. What did you expect? What did you find? What did you change?

### Part C: EDA Anti-Patterns (75+ words)
What are 3 common mistakes people make during EDA? How can these mistakes affect downstream modeling?

### Part D: Peer Review
Comment on a classmate's EDA notebook with:
- One thing they did well
- One visualization they could improve (and how)
- One additional analysis you think would be valuable
