# Project 03: Kenya Census Insights

## Overview
Analyze Kenya National Census data to extract demographic, economic, and geographic insights relevant to data-driven policy.

## Dataset Options
- [Kenya Open Data](https://www.opendata.go.ke/)
- Kenya National Bureau of Statistics publications
- World Bank Kenya data

## Key Analysis Areas

### Demographic Analysis
- Population distribution by county
- Age pyramid for Kenya
- Urban vs rural population trends

### Economic Indicators
- Household income distribution
- Employment rates by sector
- Literacy and education levels

### Geographic Analysis
- Choropleth map of population density
- Regional variations in key indicators

## Technical Requirements
- Geopandas for spatial data (bonus)
- Folium or Plotly for interactive maps
- Statistical comparisons between counties/regions

## Social Impact Focus
Your analysis should answer: **What policy interventions does the data suggest for improving quality of life in Kenya?**

## Deliverables
- Jupyter Notebook
- At least 2 maps (static or interactive)
- Policy recommendation memo (1 page)
- GitHub repository
