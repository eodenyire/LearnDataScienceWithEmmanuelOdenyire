# Project 06: Capstone – End-to-End Data Science Solution

## Overview
The final capstone project demonstrates your complete data science skillset. You will define a problem, collect data, build models, deploy a solution, and present to stakeholders.

## Requirements
See the full spec in: [assignments/Phase_6_Final_Capstone_Project.md](../assignments/Phase_6_Final_Capstone_Project.md)

## Suggested Topics by Track

### General Data Science
- Predicting smallholder farmer crop yields in Kenya
- Analyzing air quality patterns in African cities
- Customer churn prediction for a telecom company

### Data Engineering Track
- Real-time social media sentiment pipeline
- E-commerce analytics data warehouse

### MLOps Track
- End-to-end ML platform with monitoring
- AutoML tool with experiment tracking

### NLP Track
- Swahili language sentiment analysis model
- Medical chatbot for symptom triage

### Computer Vision Track
- Pothole detection for road quality assessment
- Wildlife monitoring with camera traps

## Portfolio Presentation
Present your capstone as if pitching to a company:
1. **Problem & Business Value** (2 min)
2. **Data & Approach** (3 min)
3. **Results & Demo** (5 min)
4. **Limitations & Next Steps** (2 min)
5. **Q&A** (3 min)
