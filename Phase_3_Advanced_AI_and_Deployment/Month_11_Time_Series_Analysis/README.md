# Month 11: Time Series Analysis & Forecasting

## Overview
Master techniques for analyzing and forecasting temporal data.

## Topics Covered
- Understanding time series data
- Time series decomposition
- Forecasting models: ARIMA, SARIMA, and Prophet
- Evaluating forecast accuracy
- Case studies in time series forecasting

## Key Libraries
- statsmodels
- Prophet
- pandas (time series functionality)

| Notebook | Topic |
|----------|-------|

| [Week 2: Time Series Decomposition](Week_2_Time_Series_Decomposition.ipynb) | Classical and STL decomposition; differencing |
