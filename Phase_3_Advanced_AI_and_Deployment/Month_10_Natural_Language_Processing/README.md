# Month 10: Natural Language Processing (NLP)

**Phase 3 | Month 10 | Bootcamp Days 267–294**  
**Daily Commitment:** 15 hours/day | **Total Hours:** 420 hours

## Overview

Month 10 builds comprehensive NLP skills from text preprocessing to state-of-the-art transformer models.

## Weekly Structure

| Week | Theme | Notebooks |
|------|-------|-----------|
| [Week 1 – Text Preprocessing](Week_1_Text_Preprocessing/) | Tokenization, stemming, lemmatization | Week_1_Text_Preprocessing.ipynb |
| [Week 2 – Tokenization and Embeddings](Week_2_Tokenization_and_Embeddings/) | Word2Vec, GloVe, FastText | Week_2_Tokenization_and_Embeddings.ipynb |
| [Week 3 – Sentiment Analysis](Week_3_Sentiment_Analysis/) | Text classification, fine-tuning | Week_3_Sentiment_Analysis.ipynb |
| [Week 4 – Sequence-to-Sequence Models](Week_4_Sequence_to_Sequence_Models/) | Seq2Seq, attention mechanisms | Week_4_Sequence_to_Sequence_Models.ipynb |
| [Week 5 – Transformers and BERT](Week_4_Transformers_and_BERT/) | Hugging Face, fine-tuning BERT, GPT | Week_5_Transformers_and_BERT.ipynb |

## Learning Objectives

By the end of Month 10, you will be able to:

- ✅ Preprocess text data (tokenization, stop words, stemming, lemmatization)
- ✅ Create word embeddings with Word2Vec, GloVe, and FastText
- ✅ Build text classification and sentiment analysis models
- ✅ Implement seq2seq models with attention
- ✅ Fine-tune BERT and other transformer models
- ✅ Build a complete NLP pipeline with Hugging Face
