# Phase 3: Advanced AI & Deployment — Deep Learning, NLP, Time Series & MLOps

**Duration:** 4 Months (Days 225–365 of the bootcamp)  
**Daily Commitment:** 15 hours/day  
**Total Hours:** 2,115 hours (includes graduation week)

## Overview

Phase 3 takes you to the frontier of data science and AI. You will master deep learning architectures, build NLP systems, forecast with time series models, and deploy your models to production. This phase prepares you for real industry roles.

## Phase Structure

| Month | Topic | Weeks | Days |
|-------|-------|-------|------|
| [Month 09 – Deep Learning](Month_09_Deep_Learning/) | Neural networks, TensorFlow, PyTorch, CNN, RNN | 4 | 225–252 |
| [Month 10 – Natural Language Processing](Month_10_Natural_Language_Processing/) | Text, embeddings, transformers, BERT | 4 | 253–280 |
| [Month 11 – Time Series Analysis](Month_11_Time_Series_Analysis/) | ARIMA, Prophet, LSTM forecasting | 4 | 281–308 |
| [Month 12 – End-to-End Projects & MLOps](Month_12_End_to_End_Projects/) | Deployment, Flask/FastAPI, MLflow, Docker | 4 | 309–365 |

## Daily Schedule (15 Hours/Day)

| Time Block | Activity | Duration |
|------------|----------|----------|
| 06:00–11:00 | Core deep learning notebook + reading | 5 hours |
| 11:00–16:00 | Model training, GPU experiments | 5 hours |
| 16:00–21:00 | Project development, deployment, review | 5 hours |

## Learning Objectives

By the end of Phase 3, you will be able to:

- ✅ Build CNNs for image classification and object detection
- ✅ Build RNNs and LSTMs for sequence modeling
- ✅ Fine-tune BERT and use transformer models
- ✅ Build end-to-end NLP pipelines
- ✅ Forecast time series with ARIMA, Prophet, and LSTM
- ✅ Deploy ML models with Flask, FastAPI, and Streamlit
- ✅ Track experiments with MLflow
- ✅ Containerize models with Docker
- ✅ Monitor models in production
